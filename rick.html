Rick Sanchez

Rick Sanchez is a mad scientist who is the grandfather of Morty Smith. He is a genius inventor and alcoholic who has a strained relationship with his daughter Beth. He frequently travels on adventures through space and other dimensions with his grandson Morty.

"Wubba Lubba Dub Dub!" is Rick's catchphrase that he uses throughout the series. It is a nonsense phrase that he uses to express his feeling.

Rick's dance is a popular dance that he does in the show. It is a funny dance that has become a popular meme.
