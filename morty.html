Morty Smith

Morty Smith is a good-hearted but easily distressed 14-year-old, who is easily manipulated, and can be somewhat easily provoked to anger. He is obsessed with his crush on Jessica, a fellow student at his school. Morty is often dismissed as a sidekick, but his bravery and loyalty make him a crucial part of Rick's adventures.

"Armothy" is the name Morty gives to a sentient, vengeful arm. It embodies the spirit of a warrior seeking revenge for his family's demise, granting Morty unparalleled combat skills and a deeper insight into themes of vengeance throughout their shared journey.

"Evil Morty" is a cunning antagonist, challenging Rick with his intellect and ambition. He embodies themes of power and the potential for darkness within, making him a complex and intriguing character in the series.
