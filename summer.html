Summer Smith

Summer smith is the granddaughter of Rick. She is the older sister of Morty Smith. Summer is a typical high school student who worries about improving her status among her peers. She is often dragged into Rick and Morty's adventures, and she is often the voice of reason in the family.

Summer went for 'ray surgery' to outshine her classmates but ended up starring in her own comedy of errors. Instead of turning heads, she turned her life into a blooper reel. Talk about a fitness journey with more twists than a pretzel!

In the wild cosmos of Rick and Morty, Summer emerges as the ultimate cosmic babysitter, effortlessly managing the chaos left in their wake. More than just a sidekick, she's the boss, skillfully steering their adventures back from the brink of disaster with a blend of sass and smarts.
